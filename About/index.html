Our Goal

Marko Anastasov and Darko Fabijan, two friends from university, set up the company while working at the same time on a social web startup and for clients. From the beginning we were passionate about both making software and the effects great products have on people.

We have combined those in creating Semaphore, which we launched in 2012, and transitioned to become a product company. Now that we are responsible for a service used in over 100 countries, and working as a distributed team from multiple countries, we feel like we're only getting started.
